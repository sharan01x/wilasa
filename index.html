A new concept for better living
This Bali-inspired residential complex is an oasis in a bustling part of Bangalore. All of the conveniences of a world-city like Bangalore combined with the serenity of living in the lush gardens of a gated community, Wilasa offers the best of both worlds.
Villaments are a new concept in residential living, where you get the size of a villa and the shared security and amenities of an apartment complex. Here too, we combine the best aspects of different residential styles to redefine what it means to live in luxury.

Overview

People say location is everything. We agree.

Green on Green

Wilasa is located squarely in the midst of the "green belt" of Bangalore. As such it enjoys better air quality, water availability and overall ecological balance compared with other parts of the city despite phenomenal growth in the area.

Educational Hub

Kanakapura Road is touted as the Education Corridor of the city. With over 15 renowned schools and colleges such as Kumaran's, The Valley School and Delhi Public School, Wilasa is the perfect place to raise a family.

Health Care with Reach

Wilasa is at a vantage point to easy access to many multi-speciality healthcare institutions such as Apollo, Fortis, Raghavendra multi-speciality hospital and many more. There's no need to worry if you're living with older parents who may need access to such centres.

Shopping and Entertainment

The Prestige Forum Mall with the PVR Superplex Theater is within 500 meters of Wilasa. But you also have access to many other shopping, grocery stores, coffee shops and restaurants within 15 minutes of driving distance. You'll be spoilt for choice.

Connectivity

You can walk to the Metro station from Wilasa and get to M G Road within 30 minutes. The NICE road is 10 minutes away and the Outer Ring Road is 15 minutes away. With this sort of connectivity, you can get to any part of the city conveniently.

Art, Theatre and Culture

The neighbourhood has many centres of excellence for the arts. The Khoday Centre for Performing Arts, the Art of Living Ashram, Rangashankara, Shankara Art Foundation and many more are easily accessible from Wilasa.
